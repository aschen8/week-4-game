Crystal Guessing Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals that act as buttons. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
